Camphene-Conjugated ZnO Nanoparticles

Investigated the properties and effects of camphene conjugated zinc oxide nanoparticles on Zebrafish larvae. This in-vivo study included analysis of developmental toxicity, neurotoxicity via locomotory parameters, ROS enhancement, and lipid peroxidation levels. Published in BioNanoScience

Ammo Mist - IoT Ammonia Sensor

Developed a non-electrode-based module for detecting dissolved ammonia in aquariums under the guidance of Dr. Praveen. An ESP32 module was used to transmit data to connected devices for remote monitoring. Designed a custom 3D printable housing for the sensor and module.

Clin Mist - Natural Surface Disinfectant

Along with a colleague Susikaran, we formulated a novel surface disinfectant using the antimicrobial properties of terpinen-4-ol extracted from Green tea, creating a natural alternative to sodium hypochlorite. This product was developed as part of the YUKTI Innovation challenge and also received an Institutional patent.
